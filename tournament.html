RIANSY BADMINTON OPEN 2020

RIANSY BADMINTON OPEN 2020 is a badminton tournament which is held every year in Indonesia which features high quality matches by young athletes in Indonesia that aim to promote badminton since the age of children. This tour was held in 15 major cities in Indonesia with a record 10.000 participants with age range from 11 years to 23 years. This tournament will be held on :
  • Aceh (11-18 August 2020)
  • Medan (11-18 August 2020)
  • Padang (11-18 August 2020)
  • Palembang (11-18 August 2020)
  • Lampung (11-18 August 2020)
  • Jakarta (18-25 August 2020)
  • Bandung (18-25 August 2020)
  • Surabaya (18-25 August 2020)
  • Yogyakarta (18-25 August 2020)
  • Pontianak (25-31 August 2020)
  • Palangkaraya (25-31 August 2020)
  • Banjarmasin (25-31 August 2020)
  • Samarinda (25-31 August 2020)
  • Makassar (1-7 September 2020)
  • Bali (1-7 September 2020)
