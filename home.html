Riansy Sports Center is a branch of the Riansy Group company which is an electronic trading company or often called a network shop. Since it was founded in 2015, the Riansy Group has been transformed into a unicorn that has influence not only in Indonesia but also in Southeast Asia. Until now, the Riansy Sport Center has become the most visited marketplace by the people of Indonesia to buy and sell sports equipment.

Not only focusing on being a marketplace for the people of Indonesia, Riansy Sport Center also participates in advancing sports in Indonesia by holding several sports tournaments including :

  • Riansy Football Cup
  • Riansy Badminton Open
  • Riansy Marathon
  • Riansy Fencing Tournament
  • Riansy Futsal Tournamen
